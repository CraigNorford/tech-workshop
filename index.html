About Me

15 years ago I moved to the London after growing up in Argentina. My family came over in pursuit of a better life and its important for me to get a good education. My favourite subjects are Maths, Physics and History and when I grow up I would like to become a famous mathematician and the youngest person to win a field medal. Outside of school, I love to swim and play ping pong. I also love to cook and regularly cook for my mum and younger siblings.
